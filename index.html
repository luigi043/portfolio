About Me

I am a web developer, designer, and computer engineer passionate about technology and innovation. I have solid experience in various languages and frameworks, including PHP, Laravel, Angular, React, Kotlin, Python, Java, C, C++, C#, TypeScript, as well as skills in graphic design and video editing.

I enjoy working on creative projects, ranging from web applications and mobile apps to games, always focusing on delivering efficient and high-quality solutions. My strengths include creativity, attention to detail, fast execution, and ease of working in teams, whether in-person or remotely.

Currently, I am involved in developing an e-commerce website and have completed an internship at Redshift III Innovation, Lda, as well as contributed to social projects aimed at improving the city of Beja.

I am seeking job opportunities and partnerships to grow professionally and add value to the projects I participate in. I am proactive, communicative, curious, and detail-oriented, always eager to learn and evolve.
