About Me

I'm a computer engineer with a B.Sc. from IPBeja, Portugal. I spend most of my time building full stack web apps, but I also do native Android development in Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, and I've gotten into embedded systems with Arduino and Raspberry Pi on the side.

The projects I'm most proud of are all over the place: a vehicle management platform with Spring Boot and React, health tracking apps, an insurance management system with WCF services, and custom engine control systems for my BMW E36. I like when things work well and look right.

I do my best work when I can touch every layer of a project. Database schema, backend logic, frontend, deployment. I'd rather own the whole thing than hand off pieces. I'm comfortable picking up whatever stack the job needs.

I've done internships, freelance client work, and I keep building personal projects because that's honestly how I learn best. I hold certifications in UX Design (Google), .NET Full Stack, and React development.

Before software, I worked in hospitality across Portugal. It taught me how to talk to people, stay calm when things get hectic, and notice the small stuff that actually matters.
