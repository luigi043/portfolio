About Me

I am a computer engineer and full-stack developer with a B.Sc. from IPBeja, Portugal. I work across the full technology stack — from React and Angular frontends to Spring Boot and Laravel backends, native Android apps in Kotlin with Jetpack Compose, and even embedded systems with Arduino and Raspberry Pi.

I build things that range from production-ready vehicle management platforms and health tracking apps to custom engine control systems for a turbocharged BMW E36. I care about writing clean code, designing good user experiences, and shipping work that actually solves problems.

I'm at my best when a project spans multiple disciplines — backend architecture, frontend polish, database design, and deployment. I pick up new tools quickly and I'm comfortable working solo or in a team, on-site or remote.

My background includes professional experience as a full-stack developer and intern, freelance work for clients, and a steady stream of personal projects that keep my skills sharp. I also hold certifications in UX Design (Google), .NET Full Stack, and React development.

Outside of software, I've worked in hospitality across Portugal — roles that taught me to communicate clearly, stay calm under pressure, and pay attention to the details that make a real difference for the people you're serving.
